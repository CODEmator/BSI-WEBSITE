Brilliant Speakers International
We are a professional public speaking training, mentoring and consultancy company with operational base in Port Harcourt, Rivers State

Vision

Our vision is to raise brilliant public speakers across the world through qualitative training and mentorship.

Core Objectives

  • To offer qualitative public speaking training, mentoring and consultancy services to individuals and organizations.
  • To help individuals improve their public speaking skill to professional level within record time.
  • To help individuals with the fear of public speaking overcome the challenge the easy way.
  • To help interested individuals jump start a career in public speaking the easy way.
  • To promote the art of public speaking across the world.

Core Values

  • Excellence
  • Professionalism

Mr. Henry L. Samuel, DBS

Founder | President

Mr. Henry L. Samuel, DBS, is the President and Lead coach of Brilliant Speakers International. He is a public speaking coach and trainer, orator, writer, entrepreneur and pastor. As a professional public speaking coach, Mr. Henry as he is popularly called by his students and mentees, specializes in helping aspiring public speakers and beginners of public speaking learn and master the art of public speaking to the point where they can deploy the art form brilliantly in their various work engagements and in other areas where public speaking may be necessary. With over 10 years of practical experience in public speaking cutting across different areas such as ministry, entertainment, education and training, Mr. Henry has consistently raised the bar when it comes to public speaking training and mentorship. Currently, he is unarguably one of the best public speaking coaches in Nigeria. As a passionate and experienced public speaking coach that he is, Mr. Henry believes that through the provision of qualitative training and mentorship, anyone can become brilliant at public speaking. He is a graduate of the prestigious University of Port Harcourt, choba.
