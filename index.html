Brilliant Speakers International abbreviated as BSI is a professional public speaking training, mentoring and consultancy company with operational base in Port Harcourt, Rivers State, founded by Public speaking coach and trainer Mr. Henry L. Samuel.

PUBLIC SPEAKING MENTORING PROGRAMME (PSMP)

The Public Speaking Mentoring Programme, abbreviated as PSMP is designed to introduce mentees to the field of professional public speaking and empower them with excellent public speaking skills that will make them stand out in any public speaking engagement. PSMP is a preparatory course that will successfully launch mentees into the art of professional public speaking.
