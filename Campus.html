ABOUT OUR CAMPUS CLUB

Our campus club is a platform for professional development in the area of public speaking, communication and leadership. It is an exclusive club for students of tertiary institutions that desire to develop their public speaking, communication and leadership skills. The platform also provides opportunity for social networking amongst club members.

Benefits of Membership

  • Opportunity to overcome stage fright.
  • Opportunity to develop your public speaking, communication and leadership skills.
  • Professional mentorship from a top rated public speaking coach.
  • Opportunity to connect with new people with similar interest.
  • Opportunity to enhance your social skills.

Membership Requirements

  • Interest in public speaking
  • Undergraduate of a tertiary institution.
  • Not less than 16 years of age.

Fees

Registration fee

Registration fee for new members is five thousand naira (N5, 000.00) only.


Monthly Subscription fee

The monthly subscription fee for old members is one thousand naira (N1, 000.00)

WHY YOU SHOULD JOIN OUR CAMPUS CLUB

  • Our campus club is a professional platform committed to the professional development of its members in the area of public speaking, communication and leadership.
  • At the campus club, you will receive professional help to become the best you can be in the art of public speaking.
  • Our campus club provides the opportunity to overcome your speech anxiety to help you become more confident in delivering public speeches.
  • At the campus club, you will have the opportunity to deliver live speeches and presentations at meetings to help you enhance your speaking and presentation skills.
  • At the campus club, our dedicated professional public speaking coach will be available to assist you with your public speaking needs.
  • Our campus club is also a social community and joining us will enhance your social skills greatly.
  • At the campus club, you will meet new and exciting people with whom you share similar interest.

Club Activities

Public speaking meet up: This meet up is for the development of the public speaking and communication skills of members. The meet up holds every 1st, 2nd and 3rd Sundays by 4pm at approved meet up locations.

Speaking competition: This activity is for the purpose of challenging club members to improve on their speaking skill.

Social Hang out: The purpose of this event is to encourage socialization amongst club members while providing the opportunity for club members to develop their social skills.

Leadership Retreet: This activity is for the purpose of providing mentorship to club officials thereby empowering them to become more effective in their leadership roles both within the club and outside.

End of year dinner and award party: This is an annual event scheduled for the end of each calendar year.
